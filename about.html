Hoopa-Connect

Hoopa-Connect is a local community marketplace and community platform designed for Hoopa and surrounding areas.

Marketplace

Buy, sell, trade and share items locally with community members.

Cultural Community

Support and promote local Native artwork, beadwork, carvings, regalia and handmade crafts.

Future Features

Messaging, local news, community events, requests, seller ratings and more.
