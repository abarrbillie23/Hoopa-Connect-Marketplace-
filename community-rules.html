Hoopa-Connect Rules

These rules help keep the marketplace safe, respectful and useful for everyone.

1. Be Respectful

No harassment, hate speech or abusive behavior.

2. No Scams

Fraudulent listings or misleading items are not allowed.

3. Cultural Respect

Respect Native culture, artwork and community traditions.

4. Safe Trading

Meet in safe public places when possible and use caution when trading.
